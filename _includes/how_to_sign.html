How to sign this pledge

If you would like to join us in signing this pledge, we ask that you send us your name and optional position, affiliation, and/or URL, exactly as you would like to be listed here, in one of the following ways:

  • Send us a pull request on GitHub.
  • Direct message us on Twitter (our DMs are open).
  • If neither of these options are possible, please email neveragaindottech at gmail dot com, but please note that this may delay your signature as we must validate your identity. Please send us some way for us to confirm your identity, such as a LinkedIn profile or a homepage.
